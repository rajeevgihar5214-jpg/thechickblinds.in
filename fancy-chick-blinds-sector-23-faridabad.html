Material and Weather Guidance

Correct Use of Natural Bamboo Chick Blinds

Indoor bamboo blind use

Natural bamboo blinds are suitable for living rooms, bedrooms, dining spaces, indoor windows and balcony-facing doors.

Covered balcony use

Natural bamboo can be used in covered or semi-protected balconies after checking wind and occasional rain splash.

Covered porch and patio use

Bamboo blinds may be considered for covered porches and patios after checking mounting support and weather direction.

Open outdoor use

Untreated natural bamboo may be affected by continuous rain, moisture and harsh outdoor weather.

Are natural bamboo blinds waterproof?

Untreated natural bamboo blinds should not be described as fully waterproof. PVC or another suitable weather-resistant chick blind is better for regular rain exposure.
