Material Guidance

Natural Bamboo Blinds and Waterproof PVC Options

Indoor Natural Bamboo

Natural bamboo is suitable for living rooms, bedrooms, indoor windows and protected door openings.

Covered Balcony Use

Natural bamboo can be considered where the balcony has adequate roof coverage and limited rain splash.

Direct Rain Exposure

PVC or another suitable weather-resistant material is better for regular direct rain, moisture and water splash.

Natural Bamboo Limitation

Untreated natural bamboo blinds are not fully waterproof. Continuous moisture may affect colour, shape and service life.

Wide Balcony Panels

Large openings can be divided into separate panels for easier rolling and convenient daily use.

Wind-Driven Rain

Roll-up blinds do not create a sealed wall. Strong wind and side gaps may still allow some rainwater to enter.
