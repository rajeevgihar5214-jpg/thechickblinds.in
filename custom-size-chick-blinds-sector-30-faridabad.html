Covered balcony use

Natural bamboo blinds may be suitable for covered porches and patios after checking roof coverage, wind and rainfall direction.

Untreated natural bamboo blinds should not be described as fully waterproof. Continuous moisture and direct rainfall can affect natural bamboo.

PVC or another suitable weather-resistant chick blind is generally more practical for openings that regularly receive direct rain or moisture.
