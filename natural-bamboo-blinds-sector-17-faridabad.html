Material and Weather Guidance

Natural Bamboo Blinds and Weather-Resistant Alternatives

Indoor natural bamboo blinds

Natural bamboo blinds are suitable for bedrooms, living rooms, dining spaces, indoor windows and balcony-facing doors.

Covered balcony use

Natural bamboo can be used in covered or semi-protected balconies after checking wind pressure and occasional rain splash.

Open outdoor use

Untreated natural bamboo can be affected by continuous rain, moisture and harsh outdoor weather.

Are natural bamboo blinds waterproof?

Untreated natural bamboo blinds should not be described as fully waterproof. PVC or another suitable weather-resistant blind is better for regular rain exposure.
