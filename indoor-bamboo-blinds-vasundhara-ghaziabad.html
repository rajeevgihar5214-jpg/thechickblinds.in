Material and Weather Guidance

Indoor Natural Bamboo Blinds and Waterproof Alternatives

Indoor use

Natural bamboo blinds are suitable for bedrooms, living rooms, dining areas and indoor windows where filtered light and a natural appearance are required.

Covered balcony use

Natural bamboo blinds can be used in covered balconies after checking wind pressure, mounting support and occasional rain splash.

Open outdoor use

Untreated bamboo can be affected by continuous rain, moisture and harsh outdoor exposure. Open areas require a suitable weather-resistant material.

Waterproof blind alternative

Natural bamboo blinds should not be described as fully waterproof. PVC or suitable weather-resistant chick blinds are better for openings regularly exposed to moisture.
