Do you provide vertical chick blinds in Kaushambi Ghaziabad?

Are vertical blinds and chick blinds the same product?

No. Vertical blinds normally use hanging vertical slats and are popular for offices and glass windows. Chick blinds are roll-up shades made from bamboo, PVC or similar materials.

Are vertical blinds suitable for office glass windows?

Yes. Vertical blinds are commonly used for office windows, cabins and glass partitions because the slats provide adjustable light and privacy control.

Can vertical blinds be installed on glass doors?

Vertical blinds may be installed near suitable glass-door openings after checking frame clearance, handle position, door movement and available track support.

Are bamboo chick blinds suitable for balconies?

Are untreated natural bamboo blinds fully waterproof?

No. Untreated natural bamboo blinds work best in indoor, covered or semi-protected openings. PVC or suitable weather-resistant material is better for regular direct rain and moisture.
