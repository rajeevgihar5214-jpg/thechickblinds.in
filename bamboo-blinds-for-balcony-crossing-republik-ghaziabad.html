High-Rise Balcony Guidance

Choosing Bamboo Blinds for Apartment Balconies

Check the floor height and wind

Upper-floor balconies can receive stronger wind. Large openings may need separate blind panels, suitable brackets and bottom support for practical everyday use.

Check the direction of sunlight

East-facing balconies receive stronger morning sunlight, while west-facing balconies may receive harsh afternoon glare. The bamboo weave should be selected accordingly.

Check direct rain exposure

Natural bamboo blinds are generally recommended for covered or semi-protected balconies. Untreated bamboo should not be claimed as completely waterproof.

Select practical blind sections

Dividing a large balcony opening into two or more blinds can make daily rolling, cleaning and handling more convenient.

Select the required privacy level

A closer bamboo weave or suitable backing may be considered where stronger daytime privacy and sunlight control are required.
