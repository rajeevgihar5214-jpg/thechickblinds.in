Are natural bamboo blinds suitable for balconies?

Natural bamboo blinds are suitable for covered balconies and can help filter sunlight and provide privacy. The material should be selected according to wind and rain exposure.

Do bamboo blinds block sunlight completely?

Bamboo blinds generally filter direct sunlight and reduce glare. A closer weave or suitable backing may be selected when stronger sunlight control and privacy are required.

Yes, bamboo blinds can be prepared for kitchen windows. They should be installed at a safe distance from flames, direct heat and continuous moisture.

Are untreated natural bamboo blinds fully waterproof?

No. Untreated natural bamboo blinds work best in indoor, covered or semi-protected openings. PVC or suitable weather-resistant material is better for regular direct rain and moisture.
