Outdoor Blind Selection

Choosing Bamboo Blinds for High-Rise Balconies

Check sunlight direction

West-facing balconies and windows can receive strong afternoon sunlight. A closer bamboo weave or suitable backing can be discussed when stronger light and privacy control is required.

Check wind exposure

High-rise balconies may experience strong wind. Blind width, mounting points, bottom support and section division should be selected according to the actual site conditions.

Check rain exposure

Natural bamboo blinds are generally more suitable for covered or semi-protected outdoor spaces. Product suitability should be checked when the opening receives direct rain.

Select manageable blind sections

Large balcony openings may be divided into two or more blind sections. This can make daily rolling, handling and maintenance more convenient.
