Material and Weather Guidance

Natural Bamboo Blinds for Indoor and Covered Outdoor Use

Indoor window use

Natural bamboo blinds are suitable for bedrooms, living rooms, study areas and kitchen windows where filtered daylight and a warm natural finish are required.

Covered balcony use

Covered balconies are generally suitable after checking wind exposure, opening width, mounting points and the direction of occasional rain splash.

Open balcony use

An open balcony requires careful evaluation because untreated bamboo can be affected by continuous rain, moisture and strong wind.

Waterproof blind alternatives

Untreated natural bamboo blinds should not be described as fully waterproof. PVC or suitable weather-resistant chick blinds are more practical for openings regularly exposed to moisture.
