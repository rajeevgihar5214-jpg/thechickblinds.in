Weather and Material Guidance

Natural Bamboo Blinds for Covered Porch Areas

Covered porch use

Natural bamboo blinds work well in covered porch areas after checking the direction of sunlight, wind and occasional rain splash.

Semi-protected porch use

A semi-protected opening should be inspected carefully because strong wind or side rain may affect the blind and its operating ropes.

Open outdoor use

Untreated natural bamboo can be affected by continuous rain, moisture and harsh outdoor exposure. Open areas require a more suitable weather-resistant option.

Waterproof alternatives

Natural bamboo blinds should not be described as fully waterproof. PVC or suitable weather-resistant chick blinds are more practical for openings regularly exposed to rain.
