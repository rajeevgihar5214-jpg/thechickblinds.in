Material and Weather Guidance

Cane and cane-look blinds are generally suitable for living rooms, bedrooms, dining areas and protected indoor windows.

Indoor natural bamboo blinds

Natural bamboo blinds are suitable for indoor windows, protected doors and other openings without regular moisture.

Covered balcony installation

Bamboo blinds may suit covered porches and patios where the material remains protected from regular direct rainfall.

Are natural cane or bamboo blinds waterproof?

Untreated natural cane and bamboo blinds are not fully waterproof. Continuous moisture and direct rain can affect natural materials.

Rain-exposed openings

PVC or another suitable weather-resistant chick blind is more practical for openings that regularly receive direct rain or moisture.
