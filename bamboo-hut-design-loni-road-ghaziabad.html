Site and Safety Checks

Important Points Before Bamboo Hut Installation

Terrace load and surface suitability

A terrace structure should proceed only after the property owner confirms that the roof, base and proposed mounting points are suitable.

Society, landlord or property permission

Residents should obtain any necessary society, landlord or property-management approval before fabrication begins.

Wind and rain exposure

The design should consider wind direction, rain, roof slope, side exposure and drainage, especially on open terraces.

Ground foundation and anchoring

Garden huts require a stable base and suitable anchoring. Uneven, loose or waterlogged ground may require preparation.

Electrical and fire safety

Lighting, wiring, heaters and cooking equipment should be planned by qualified professionals and kept safely away from untreated natural material.

Periodic bamboo maintenance

Outdoor bamboo structures require cleaning, inspection, protective treatment and timely repairs depending on their weather exposure.
