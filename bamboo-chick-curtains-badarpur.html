Natural Bamboo Curtains and Waterproof Alternatives

Indoor bamboo curtain use

Natural bamboo curtains are suitable for living rooms, bedrooms, windows, doors and other indoor openings.

Covered balcony use

Natural bamboo curtains can be installed in covered balconies after checking wind exposure and occasional rain splash.

Open balcony use

Untreated natural bamboo may be affected by continuous rain, moisture and harsh outdoor exposure.

Are natural bamboo curtains waterproof?

Untreated natural bamboo curtains should not be described as fully waterproof. PVC or another suitable weather-resistant chick blind is better for regular rain exposure.
