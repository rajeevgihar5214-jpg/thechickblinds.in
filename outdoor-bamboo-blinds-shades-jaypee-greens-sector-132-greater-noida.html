Outdoor Blind Selection

Choosing Bamboo Blinds for Outdoor-Facing Spaces

Check sunlight direction

West-facing balconies and windows may receive stronger afternoon sunlight. A closer bamboo weave or suitable backing may provide better glare and privacy control.

Check wind exposure

High-rise balconies can receive strong wind. The blind size, section width, bottom support and mounting points should be selected according to site conditions.

Check rain and moisture exposure

Natural bamboo blinds are best suited to covered or semi-protected outdoor spaces. Product suitability should be confirmed when the opening receives direct rain.

Select the correct operating method

A manual bamboo roll up blind offers simple daily control. Large openings may be divided into multiple sections for easier operation and balanced fitting.
