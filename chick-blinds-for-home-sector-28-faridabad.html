Material and Weather Guidance

Natural Bamboo Blinds and Waterproof Alternatives

Indoor natural bamboo blinds

Natural bamboo blinds are suitable for living rooms, bedrooms, dining spaces, indoor windows and protected door openings.

Covered balcony use

Natural bamboo can be used in covered or semi-protected balconies after checking wind pressure and occasional rain splash.

Covered porch and patio use

Bamboo blinds may be considered for covered porches and patios after checking the roof coverage, wind and rain direction.

Are natural bamboo blinds waterproof?

Untreated natural bamboo blinds should not be described as fully waterproof. Continuous moisture and direct rainfall can affect natural bamboo.

Rain-exposed openings

PVC or another suitable weather-resistant chick blind is generally more practical for openings that regularly receive direct rain or moisture.
