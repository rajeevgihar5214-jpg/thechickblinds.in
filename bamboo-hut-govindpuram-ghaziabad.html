Site and Safety Guidance

Important Checks Before Bamboo Hut Installation

Terrace structure and load

Terrace installations should proceed only after the property owner confirms that the roof, mounting points and proposed structure are suitable.

Society or property permission

Apartment residents should obtain any necessary society, landlord or property-management approval before fabrication or installation begins.

Wind and weather exposure

The design should consider local wind, rain direction, drainage and exposure because open terraces may require stronger support than protected gardens.

Ground and foundation conditions

Garden and farmhouse huts require a stable base and suitable anchoring. Loose, uneven or waterlogged ground should be addressed before installation.

Electrical and fire safety

Electrical lights, wiring, heaters or cooking equipment should be planned and installed safely by qualified professionals, away from untreated natural materials.

Periodic bamboo maintenance

Natural bamboo structures require periodic checking, cleaning, protective treatment and timely repair depending on outdoor exposure.
