Porch Blind Planning

How to Choose Bamboo Blinds for a Covered Porch

Check Roof Coverage

Natural bamboo is more suitable when the porch has adequate overhead protection from regular direct rainfall.

Check Sunlight Direction

West-facing entrances may require a closer bamboo weave or suitable backing for stronger afternoon sunlight.

Check Wind Exposure

Open porch sides may require narrower blind panels and suitable lower support to control excessive movement.

Maintain Door Access

The entrance door section should have a separate blind so daily movement remains convenient.

Divide Wide Openings

Wide verandas can be divided into several blind panels for easier rolling, balanced weight and cleaning.

Select Privacy Level

A closer bamboo weave can offer better daytime privacy than a widely spaced decorative weave.
