Material Information

Wooden Chick Blinds vs Natural Bamboo Blinds

Wooden-look chick blinds

Wooden-look chick blinds use bamboo, wooden strips or wood-finish woven material to create a natural appearance with convenient roll-up operation.

Natural bamboo blinds

Natural bamboo blinds use woven bamboo material and are popular for balconies, windows, porches and traditionally styled interiors.

Solid wooden blinds

Solid wooden slat blinds are a separate product category. The material, weight, operating system and fitting requirements differ from lightweight roll-up bamboo chick blinds.

Waterproof suitability

Untreated natural bamboo or wooden blinds should not be described as fully waterproof. PVC or suitable weather-resistant materials are better for openings receiving regular moisture or rain splash.
