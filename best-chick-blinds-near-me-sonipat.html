Material Guidance

Natural Bamboo Blinds vs Waterproof PVC Blinds

Indoor Natural Bamboo

Natural bamboo is suitable for living rooms, bedrooms, indoor windows, dining areas and protected door openings.

Covered Balconies

Natural bamboo can be considered where the balcony has adequate roof coverage and receives limited rain splash.

Covered Porches and Patios

Bamboo blinds may suit covered porches, verandas and patios after checking wind and moisture exposure.

Direct Rain Areas

PVC or another suitable weather-resistant material is better for openings receiving regular direct rain or water splash.

Natural Bamboo Limitation

Untreated natural bamboo blinds are not fully waterproof. Regular moisture can affect their colour, shape and life.

Wind-Driven Rain

Roll-up blinds do not create a sealed wall. Strong wind and side gaps may still allow some rainwater to enter.
