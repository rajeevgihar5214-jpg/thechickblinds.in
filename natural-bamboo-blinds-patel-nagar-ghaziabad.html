Natural Bamboo Material Guidance

Indoor, Covered Outdoor and Weather-Exposed Use

Natural bamboo blinds for indoor windows

Natural bamboo blinds work well on bedroom, living-room, study and kitchen windows where a warm decorative finish and filtered daylight are required.

Natural bamboo blinds for covered balconies

Covered balconies are generally suitable for natural bamboo blinds after checking wind exposure, mounting support and the direction of occasional rain splash.

Natural bamboo blinds for open balconies

Open balconies require careful evaluation because untreated bamboo can be affected by continuous rain, moisture and strong outdoor wind.

Waterproof and PVC alternatives

PVC or suitable weather-resistant chick blinds are a better option where the opening regularly receives direct rain or moisture. Natural bamboo should not be described as fully waterproof.
