Material Selection Guide

PVC Chick Blinds vs Natural Bamboo Blinds

PVC Blind Material

PVC is suitable for balconies, outdoor windows and other openings exposed to regular moisture or rain splash.

Bamboo-Look PVC Option

Selected PVC or synthetic woven products can provide a bamboo-inspired appearance with improved moisture resistance.

Natural Bamboo Material

Natural bamboo provides an earthy woven appearance and works best in indoor, covered or semi-protected spaces.

Direct Rain Exposure

Open balconies receiving regular direct rain should generally use PVC or another appropriate weather-resistant material.

Covered Balcony Use

Natural bamboo may be considered when the balcony has suitable roof coverage and receives only occasional rain splash.

Weather Limitation

Roll-up blinds do not form a completely sealed wall. Strong wind and side gaps may still allow some rainwater to enter.
