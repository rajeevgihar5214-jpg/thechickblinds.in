Are natural bamboo blinds waterproof for Gurugram balconies?

Untreated natural bamboo blinds are not fully waterproof. They are better for indoor, covered or semi-protected openings. Regular rain-facing areas should be assessed for PVC or another weather-resistant material.

Can you make custom size bamboo blinds in Gurugram?

Yes. Custom size bamboo blinds and common sizes such as bamboo blinds 4x6 feet can be prepared after final width, height, panel division, mounting support and operating clearance are checked.

What affects bamboo chick blinds price in Gurugram?

Bamboo chick blinds price depends on measured size, weave, lining, panel count, operating mechanism, edge finishing, fitting hardware, access and the selected material.

What are the Gurugram inspection charges?

Inspection and final measurement charges generally range from ₹300 to ₹500 depending on travel, parking, society access, number of openings and site conditions.
