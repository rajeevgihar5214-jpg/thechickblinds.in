Outdoor Material Guidance

Natural Bamboo vs Weather-Resistant Blinds

Natural Bamboo Blinds

Natural bamboo provides an earthy woven finish and is best for indoor, covered or semi-protected openings.

Covered Balcony Use

Natural bamboo may be suitable when the balcony has adequate roof protection and receives only occasional rain splash.

Covered Porch Use

Bamboo blinds can be installed on covered porches after checking wind pressure and the direction of seasonal rain.

Regular Direct Rain

PVC or another suitable weather-resistant material is better for openings receiving regular direct rain and moisture.

Weather-Resistant Bamboo Look

Selected PVC or synthetic woven materials may provide a bamboo-inspired appearance with improved moisture resistance.

Outdoor Limitations

Roll-up blinds do not form a sealed wall. Strong wind and side gaps may still allow some rainwater to enter.
