Balcony Blind Selection

Choosing Bamboo Blinds for Indirapuram High-Rise Apartments

Check the balcony direction

East-facing balconies receive stronger morning light, while west-facing balconies can receive harsh afternoon sunlight. The bamboo weave should be selected accordingly.

Check wind exposure

Upper-floor balconies may experience stronger wind. Large openings can be divided into smaller blind sections for easier handling and more balanced fitting.

Check direct rain exposure

Natural bamboo blinds are generally more suitable for covered or semi-protected balconies. A suitable outdoor option should be selected when the opening receives direct rain.

Select suitable privacy control

A closer bamboo weave or optional backing may be considered where stronger privacy and sunlight control are required.
