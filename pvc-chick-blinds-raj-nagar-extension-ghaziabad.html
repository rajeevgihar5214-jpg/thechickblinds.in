High-Rise Balcony Guidance

Choosing PVC Chick Blinds for Apartment Balconies

Check floor height and wind

Higher floors may experience stronger wind. The blind width, bracket quality, bottom support and number of sections should be selected accordingly.

Check rain direction

PVC blinds handle moisture better than untreated bamboo, but wind-driven rain may still enter through the sides of an open balcony.

Check sunlight direction

West-facing balconies may require a denser blind or suitable backing for stronger sunlight and privacy control.

Choose manageable sections

Large openings can be divided into separate blinds so daily rolling, cleaning and handling remain convenient.
