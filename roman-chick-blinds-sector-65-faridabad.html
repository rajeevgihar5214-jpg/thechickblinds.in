Material and Weather Guidance

Correct Use of Roman and Natural Bamboo Blinds

Indoor Roman Blind Installation

Roman-style blinds are suitable for living rooms, bedrooms, dining spaces and other protected indoor windows.

Indoor Natural Bamboo Blinds

Natural bamboo blinds are suitable for indoor windows, protected doors and openings without regular moisture.

Covered Balcony Installation

Natural bamboo may be used in covered or semi-protected balconies after checking wind and occasional rain splash.

Covered Porch and Patio Use

Bamboo blinds may suit covered porches and patios where the material remains protected from regular direct rainfall.

Are Natural Bamboo Blinds Waterproof?

Untreated natural bamboo blinds are not fully waterproof. Continuous moisture and direct rain can affect the material. PVC or another suitable weather-resistant blind is better for regular rain exposure.
