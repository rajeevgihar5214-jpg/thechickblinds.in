Material Selection Guide

PVC Chick Blinds vs Natural Bamboo Blinds

PVC Chick Blinds

PVC is generally more practical for balconies and outdoor openings exposed to regular rain, water splash or moisture.

Natural Bamboo Blinds

Natural bamboo blinds provide an earthy woven appearance and work best indoors or in covered and semi-protected spaces.

Direct Rain Exposure

Open balconies facing regular direct rain generally require PVC or another suitable weather-resistant blind material.

Covered Balcony Use

Natural bamboo may be considered when the balcony has adequate roof coverage and receives only occasional rain splash.

Wind-Driven Rain

No roll-up blind creates a completely sealed wall. Side gaps and strong wind may still allow some water to enter.

Site-Specific Selection

The final material should be selected after checking the opening direction, roof coverage, wind and installation support.
