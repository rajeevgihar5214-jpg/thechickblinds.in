Porch Blind Selection

Choosing Bamboo Blinds for Covered Porch Areas

Check the mounting support

The ceiling, wall or frame should provide suitable support for the bamboo blind brackets. The fitting method is selected after checking the actual opening.

Check sunlight direction

Porch areas receiving direct afternoon sunlight may require a closer bamboo weave or an optional backing for stronger glare control and privacy.

Check wind and rain exposure

Natural bamboo blinds are better suited to covered or semi-protected porch areas. Direct rain and strong wind exposure should be checked before installation.

Select manageable blind sections

Large porch openings may be divided into two or more sections so that everyday rolling and handling remain convenient.

Can bamboo blinds be installed in an open porch?

Natural bamboo blinds should not automatically be treated as fully waterproof. A suitable outdoor option should be selected when the porch receives moisture or occasional rain exposure.
